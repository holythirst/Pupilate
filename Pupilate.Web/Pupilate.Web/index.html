Welcome - About Pupilate

Pupilate® is an online based educational ERP system designed and developed by Automate Universal Innovations (Nig.) Limited - an ICT company with a vision and mission to help people of all works of life improve the way they do things with much more efficiency through Information and Communication Technology.
Pupilate strongly makes the process of record keeping, retrieval and backup of data an easy and enjoyable task and experience. It also brings great reliability and flexibility into the running of schools with the provision of different easy-to-use and efficient utilities to support and make the school management as easy and stress free as possible.
